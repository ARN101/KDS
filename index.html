About Us

KUET Debating Society is the premier platform for intellectual discourse at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology. We nurture critical thinking, public speaking, and the art of persuasion among the students of KUET.

Founded with the vision of creating confident and articulate leaders, KDS has been a cornerstone of co-curricular excellence on campus. Our members regularly participate in national and inter-university debate competitions, bringing pride to the institution.

What We Do

Weekly Sessions

Regular practice sessions to sharpen argumentation and speaking skills through structured debates.

Tournaments

We participate in and host inter-university debate tournaments in both Bangla and English formats.

Workshops

Skill-building workshops on public speaking, critical analysis, and effective communication.
